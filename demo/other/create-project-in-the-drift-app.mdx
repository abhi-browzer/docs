The Drift App is a project management platform designed to help teams organize work through customizable templates, team collaboration, and integrated workflows. Creating a project establishes the foundation for your team's work—it defines the project structure (via templates like Kanban boards), invites collaborators, sets access controls, and initializes tracking features like milestones and tasks. This guided setup wizard walks you through each configuration step, ensuring your project is properly configured for your team's needs before you begin execution.

## Prerequisites

- Access to the Drift App at drift-gilt.vercel.app with an active account
- Project name and description prepared (optional but recommended)
- Email addresses of team members you plan to invite
- Understanding of your project's privacy requirements (private vs. shared access)
- Integration preferences identified (e.g., third-party tool connections)

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="I completed the wizard but my project was created in a default 'Draft' project instead of as a new standalone project. What happened?">
    This occurs when the project creation process encounters an error or incomplete submission. Verify that you clicked the final 'Create project' button on the Review step, not an intermediate 'Next' button. If the issue persists, check your browser console for errors and ensure your account has permission to create new projects. Contact support if the problem continues.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="I see an error 'Failed to create Vercel project: Your Team encountered an unknown problem.' What should I do?">
    This is a server-side error typically caused by temporary service issues or account-level restrictions. Retry the project creation after waiting a few minutes. If you still encounter the error, reach out to the Drift support team with your account details and the exact error message. This issue may affect multiple users simultaneously.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="I cannot find the 'Add to Project' button or option when trying to add items to my new project. Where is it?">
    Ensure you have completed the project creation wizard fully and the project has been saved. The 'Add to Project' option typically appears in task or item creation dialogs. If you do not see it, refresh the page or navigate away and back to the project dashboard to ensure the project is fully initialized.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="When I try to add team members, their email addresses are not being recognized. What could be wrong?">
    Verify that email addresses are spelled correctly and that team members have existing accounts in the Drift App or organization. Some systems require team members to activate their accounts before they can be assigned to projects. Confirm your organization's member management settings if you are an administrator.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="The integration connection buttons did not work. How do I retry connecting integrations?">
    If a 'Connect' button does not respond, you may have encountered a popup blocker or temporary service interruption. Disable popup blockers for drift-gilt.vercel.app and retry. You can also complete project creation and configure integrations afterward through the project settings—they are not mandatory to create the project.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
