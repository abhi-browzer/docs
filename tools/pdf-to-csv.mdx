The PDF to CSV tool lets you pull tables and structured data out of any PDF and download the result as a CSV file ready for Excel, Google Sheets, or Numbers. Choose **Standard** mode for fast, free processing of digitally-created PDFs, or switch to **AI-Powered** mode when you need to handle scanned documents and complex layouts.

## How to Use

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the tool">
    Navigate to [https://newtools.space/pdf-to-csv](https://newtools.space/pdf-to-csv) in your browser — no installation required.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose your mode">
    Select **Standard** for a regular digital PDF, or **AI-Powered** if your file is scanned or has a complex layout. AI mode requires a free account and allows 3 conversions per day.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload your PDF">
    Drag and drop your file onto the upload area, or click to browse. Files must be 20 MB or smaller.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Download your CSV">
    Once processing is complete, download your CSV file and open it in your spreadsheet app of choice.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## When to Use Each Mode

Use **Standard** mode for the vast majority of PDFs you encounter day-to-day — reports, invoices, spreadsheets, and any document that was digitally created. It is completely free, requires no sign-in, and places no cap on how many files you convert.

Switch to **AI-Powered** mode when Standard mode produces blank or garbled output. This typically happens with scanned documents (i.e., PDFs that are essentially images of paper) or files with unusual multi-column layouts. You will need to sign in with a free NewTools account, and each account receives 3 AI conversions per day at no cost.

<Note>
  Your PDF is never stored on NewTools servers. Standard mode sends your file to the backend for pdfplumber processing and discards it immediately after conversion. AI mode sends your file to the NewTools AI backend and likewise discards it once the CSV has been generated.
</Note>

## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Is my PDF uploaded to a server?">
    Yes — processing happens server-side so the file is transmitted over a secure connection. In **Standard** mode your PDF is sent to the NewTools backend for pdfplumber processing but is **not stored** and is discarded immediately. In **AI-Powered** mode your PDF is sent to the NewTools AI backend and is also discarded as soon as your CSV is ready.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What types of PDFs work best with Standard mode?">
    Standard mode works best with **digitally-created PDFs** — documents that were exported or saved directly from software like Word, Excel, Google Docs, or a web browser. If your PDF was produced by scanning a physical page, the text is stored as an image and pdfplumber cannot read it. Use AI-Powered mode for scanned documents.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What is the file size limit?">
    Both Standard and AI-Powered modes accept files up to **20 MB**. If your PDF exceeds this limit, consider splitting it into smaller sections before uploading.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I get more AI conversions?">
    Every free NewTools account includes **3 AI-Powered conversions per day**, and the count resets at midnight. For higher volume needs, check the [NewTools website](https://newtools.space) for any available paid plans or increased quota options.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
