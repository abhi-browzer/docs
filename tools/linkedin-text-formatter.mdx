The **LinkedIn Text Formatter** lets you style your LinkedIn posts, comments, headlines, and summaries with bold, italic, strikethrough, and other rich text effects — for free, with no sign-up required. LinkedIn doesn't support native rich text formatting, so this tool uses special Unicode characters that visually mimic bold, italic, and other styles while displaying correctly on every device and platform. Just type or paste your text, pick a style, and copy the formatted result straight into LinkedIn.

## How to Use

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the tool">
    Go to [https://newtools.space/linkedin-text-formatter](https://newtools.space/linkedin-text-formatter) in your browser.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a formatting style">
    Select the style you want to apply — Bold, Italic, Bold Italic, Underline, Strikethrough, or Monospace.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Type or paste your text">
    Enter the text you want to format in the input field, or paste it from your clipboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="See the formatted output instantly">
    The formatted Unicode text appears in real time as you type, so you can preview exactly how it will look.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Copy and paste into LinkedIn">
    Click **Copy**, then paste your formatted text directly into a LinkedIn post, comment, message, headline, or summary.
  </Step>
</Steps>
