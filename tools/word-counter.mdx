The Word Counter gives you a complete breakdown of any block of text the moment you type or paste it in. You can see your word count, character totals (with and without spaces), sentence and paragraph counts, and an estimated reading time — all updating live, with no button to click and no account to create.

## What It Counts

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Words" icon="align-left">
    Total number of words in your text, counted in real time as you type or paste.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Characters" icon="text-size">
    Total character count **including** spaces, punctuation, and all other characters.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Characters (no spaces)" icon="font">
    Character count **excluding** spaces — useful for platforms with character limits that ignore whitespace.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Sentences" icon="list-ol">
    Number of sentences detected, based on standard punctuation (periods, exclamation marks, question marks).
  </Card>
  <Card title="Paragraphs" icon="paragraph">
    Number of paragraphs, determined by line breaks in your text.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Reading Time" icon="clock">
    Estimated reading time calculated at an average pace of **200 words per minute**.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## How to Use

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the tool">
    Go to [https://newtools.space/word-counter](https://newtools.space/word-counter) in any browser — desktop or mobile.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Paste or type your text">

  </Step>

    All six statistics update in real time as you edit. There is nothing to submit — your results are always current.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How is reading time calculated?">
    Reading time is based on an average adult silent reading speed of **200 words per minute**. Divide your total word count by 200 to get the estimate in minutes. For example, a 1,000-word document shows an estimated reading time of 5 minutes. This is a general guide — your actual pace may vary depending on the complexity of the content.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Does this work on mobile?">
    Yes. The Word Counter is fully responsive and works on phones and tablets. You can paste text from your clipboard or type directly in the text area on any mobile browser without needing to install an app.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Is there a character or word limit?">
    There is no enforced word or character limit. You can paste in long documents, essays, or articles and the counter will handle them without issue. Very large blocks of text may cause a brief processing delay depending on your device.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Is it free?">
    Yes, completely. The Word Counter is free to use with no sign-up, no account, and no ads. All six statistics are available to everyone at no cost.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
