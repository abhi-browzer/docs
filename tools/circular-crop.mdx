The **Circular Crop** tool lets you turn any image into a perfectly circular cutout with a transparent background — all without leaving your browser. Upload a PNG, JPG, or WEBP, drag to reposition, zoom in or out, and download a clean transparent PNG in seconds. Because everything is processed locally via the HTML Canvas API, your image is 100% private and never touches a server.

## How to Use

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the tool">
    Go to [https://newtools.space/circular-crop](https://newtools.space/circular-crop) in your browser.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload your image">
    Tap the upload area or drag and drop any PNG, JPG, or WEBP file onto the page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Position and zoom">
    Drag the image to frame exactly what you want inside the circle. Use the zoom slider — or pinch with two fingers on mobile — to get the perfect crop area.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Crop the image">
    Click the **Crop** button to generate your circular image preview.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Download your transparent PNG">
    Click **Download** to save the result as a PNG with a transparent circular cutout, ready to use in any design, profile, or presentation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Re-crop if needed (optional)">
    Not quite right? Click **Re-crop** to go back and adjust the position or zoom without having to re-upload your file.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Supported Formats

You can upload images in any of the following formats — there are no size restrictions:

- **PNG** — including images with existing transparency
- **JPG / JPEG** — photos and compressed images
- **WEBP** — modern web-optimized images

## Output

The tool always outputs a **transparent PNG**. The circular crop is applied as a mask, so everything outside the circle becomes fully transparent. This makes the result perfect for profile pictures, logos, overlays, and anywhere you need a clean circular cutout.
