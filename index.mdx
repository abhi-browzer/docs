Newtools is a growing collection of free, browser-based utilities designed to make everyday tasks faster and simpler. Whether you need to convert a scanned PDF into a spreadsheet, format messy JSON, or crop a profile photo into a perfect circle, Newtools handles it entirely in your browser — no account required, no files stored, no data sent anywhere you didn't ask. This documentation covers every tool available on [newtools.space](https://newtools.space), explains how each one works, and helps you get the most out of them.

## Quickstart: Convert a PDF to CSV

See how easy it is to use a Newtools utility — no installs, no accounts, no waiting.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the PDF to CSV tool">
    Navigate to [newtools.space/pdf-to-csv](https://newtools.space/pdf-to-csv). The tool loads entirely in your browser — nothing is installed on your machine.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload your PDF">
    Click **Choose File** and select the PDF containing the table you want to extract. For standard PDFs, pdfplumber is used automatically. If your file is a scanned document, enable the **AI-Powered** option to use optical character recognition.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Preview and adjust">
    Once processing is complete, a preview of the extracted table appears on screen. Review the columns and rows to confirm the data looks correct before downloading.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Download your CSV">
    Click **Download CSV** to save the file to your device. Your PDF is processed and the result returned immediately — nothing is stored or retained on our end once the CSV is delivered.
  </Step>
</Steps>
