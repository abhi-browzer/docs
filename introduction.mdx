Newtools is a growing suite of everyday utilities you can use directly in your browser — completely free, with no account required. Whether you need to convert a PDF, format some JSON, or crop an image into a circle, every tool is ready to use the moment you open the page. There's nothing to install, nothing to sign up for, and nothing stored on our end.

## Why Newtools

Newtools exists because useful tools shouldn't come with a paywall or a sign-up form. Every utility on the platform is free to use and is built with your privacy in mind. Most tools run entirely in your browser, so your data never leaves your device. Where a server is involved — such as PDF to CSV — nothing you send is stored, logged, or used for any purpose beyond returning your result. You get what you came for, and nothing else follows you out the door.

<Note>
  Standard tools require no account — just open the page and start working. The AI-powered PDF conversion gives you 3 free conversions per day and requires a sign-in purely for rate-limiting purposes.
</Note>
